CAREER GUIDANCE DOCUMENT: INTERVIEW PREPARATION GUIDE

TECHNICAL INTERVIEW PREPARATION
DATA STRUCTURES AND ALGORITHMS
• Master: Arrays, Linked Lists, Trees, Graphs, Hash Tables
• Practice: LeetCode (150+ problems), HackerRank, CodeSignal
• Focus Areas: Time/Space Complexity, Dynamic Programming, Recursion
• Tip: Explain your thought process clearly while coding
SYSTEM DESIGN
• Learn: Scalability, Load Balancing, Caching, Database Design
• Practice: Design Twitter, Design URL Shortener, Design Netflix
• Key Concepts: CAP Theorem, Microservices, API Design
• Resources: System Design Primer, Grokking System Design
PROGRAMMING LANGUAGES
• Be proficient in at least one: Python, Java, C++, JavaScript
• Know: OOP concepts, Design Patterns, Best Practices
• Practice: Code reviews, Open source contributions

BEHAVIORAL INTERVIEW PREPARATION
STAR METHOD (Situation, Task, Action, Result)
• Prepare 5-7 stories covering different competencies
• Include: Leadership, Conflict Resolution, Failure, Success
COMMON QUESTIONS
• Tell me about yourself (2-minute pitch)
• Why this company? (Research company values)
• Biggest weakness? (Show self-awareness and growth)
• Where do you see yourself in 5 years?
LEADERSHIP PRINCIPLES (Amazon-style)
• Customer Obsession: Put customer first
• Ownership: Think long-term, act on behalf of company
• Invent and Simplify: Seek innovation
• Learn and Be Curious: Never stop learning
• Hire and Develop the Best: Raise performance bar

INTERVIEW DAY TIPS

BEFORE THE INTERVIEW:
• Research the company thoroughly
• Review your resume and projects
• Prepare questions to ask interviewers
• Test your setup (for virtual interviews)
• Get good sleep, eat well

DURING THE INTERVIEW:
• Arrive 10 minutes early (or login early for virtual)
• Dress professionally
• Make eye contact and smile
• Listen carefully to questions
• Ask clarifying questions
• Think aloud while solving problems
• Be honest if you don't know something

AFTER THE INTERVIEW:
• Send thank-you email within 24 hours
• Reflect on what went well and areas to improve
• Follow up appropriately on timeline

TECHNICAL SCREENING TIPS:
• Clarify requirements before coding
• Start with brute force, then optimize
• Test your code with examples
• Discuss trade-offs
• Handle edge cases

SALARY NEGOTIATION:
• Research market rates (Glassdoor, Levels.fyi)
• Know your worth and minimum acceptable offer
• Consider total compensation (base, bonus, equity, benefits)
• Be professional and grateful
• Get offers in writing

RED FLAGS TO WATCH:
• High turnover rate
• Unclear job responsibilities
• Poor communication during process
• Unrealistic expectations
• Toxic culture indicators
