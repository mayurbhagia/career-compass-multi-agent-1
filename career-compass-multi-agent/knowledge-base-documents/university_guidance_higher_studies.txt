APPLICATION REQUIREMENTS

STANDARDIZED TESTS:
• GRE: Most US universities (some waiving post-COVID)
• TOEFL: 100+ (iBT) or IELTS: 7.0+ for non-native speakers

DOCUMENTS NEEDED:
• Statement of Purpose (SOP)
• Letters of Recommendation (3)
• Resume/CV
• Transcripts
• Research papers (if applicable)

TIMELINE:
• June-August: Shortlist universities, prepare for GRE/TOEFL
• September-October: Take GRE/TOEFL, draft SOP
• November: Request recommendation letters
• December-January: Submit applications
• March-April: Receive decisions
• August-September: Start program
